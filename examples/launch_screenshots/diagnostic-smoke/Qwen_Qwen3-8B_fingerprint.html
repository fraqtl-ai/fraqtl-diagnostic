About

This diagnostic measures information-theoretic compressibility from the input covariance spectrum of each layer. It does not perform compression — it reports the ceiling and shape. The fraQtl compression engine uses this fingerprint plus additional engineering (sign correction, per-model calibration) to get closer to the ceiling in practice.
